Jangi-Gacha Policy!

  • Genre: Gacha, Parameter Managing
  • Platform: Mobile
  • Development Period: 2018. 12. ~ 2019. 2. (3 months)

Screenshots of Jangi-Gacha Policy

Overview

"Jangi-Gacha Policy!" is a gacha game with madness, mainly focusing on parodying about the gacha trend which makes user spend enormous amount of money. The term "Jangi (장기)" means both "Organ" and "Long-term" in Korean; hence the title means "Long-term Gacha Policy" while "Harvesting Organ To Do Gacha".

In short: You, the President of Earth Nation, have to harvest people's organ and sell it to do your favorite GACHA. Overcome the probability of 0.0001% and get the shiny new "SSSSR" card!
